How To Make

Rainbow Crepe Cake

Can Be Birthday Cake?

Crepe cake, also known as gâteau de crêpe or mille crêpe, is comprised over many thin layers of crepe sandwiched between pastry cream. Crepes are great for any meal because they are so versatile. Make a savory bacon, egg, and cheddar filled crepe for breakfast. Then, a sun dried tomato with feta and arugula crepe for lunch.

And in between all those savory crepes you can have plenty of sweet dessert crepes! This crepe cake can also be used as a birthday cake, because the cake's soft texture and sweet taste will certainly spoil the tongue of anyone who eats it.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 65 g sugar
  • 1 g salt
  • 5 g vanilla extract
  • 250 g cake flour
  • 625 g milk
  • 90 g Melted unsalted butter
  • Food coloring (orange, yellow, green, blue)
  • Whipped cream

Materials

  • Pan/Teflon
  • Stove
  • Bowl
  • Whisk
  • Measuring cup
  • Filter
  • Spatula

A very thin flat cake, similar to a pancake, that is served as a base for a variety of sweet toppings or as a food wrap to be filled with rich and savory ingredients rolled inside the crêpe. A typical recipe for crêpe batter will combine flour with egg yolks or whole eggs, and milk or cream. A crêpe can be made as a sweet or a savory crêpe, depending on the ingredients in the recipe. Sweet crepes have sugar and other flavorings that provide a sweet taste to the crêpe batter.

How To Make?

Rainbow Crepe Cake

First Step?

Add sugar, salt, vanilla extract to the egg and mix it well

Second Step?

Sift the flour and add to the mixture

Third Step?

Add just a little of the milk (125 g) and mix until the flour is completely mixed. After that, add all the remaining milk (500 g) and stir well

Fourth Step?

Add melted unsalted butter and mix

Fifth Step?

Filter the batter, then cover it and put it in the fridge for about 1 hour

Sixth Step?

Mix the batter again when you get it out from the fridge and divide it into 4 bowls

Seventh Step?

Add a little color to each bowl and stir well

[ if you add a little white pigment and mix together, you can get a more beautiful pastel color ]

Eighth Step?

Heat the pan, then apply it with a little butter to make 6-7 crepes per color

[To make the crepe, don't make it too thick. So that when all the crepes are stacked, it can make the cake look good]

Ninth Step?

Put one chilled crepe and put the cream on top, do it over and over

[I apply cream on the edges and top of the crepe cake]

Tenth Step?

Add the toppings according to your taste

Crepe

This is a simple recipe with a fairly inexpensive list of ingredients. If you don’t have vanilla beans, you can easily swap in vanilla extract. As for the other items, they are staples you’d normally have in your pantry. Once you get the technique of swirling the batter in the crepe pan, making this cake is a breeze.
